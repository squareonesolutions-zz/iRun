Privacy Policy

Our policy is simple. We will protect your personal and identifiable information aggressively and will restrict any access to it both internally and externally. We do not “sell” your information to third parties or entities for any reason. Your information is stored within our database for the sole purpose of providing you with the highest quality service and products available. Period.

Any personally-identifiable information is only accessed by a very select few people within our company who have been personally screened through extensive background investigations and clearance practices to ensure that your information will remain safe. We take security as seriously as you do… maybe more.

Our website uses eCommerce shopping software that is fully PCI/PA-DSS compliant and is secured using a Comodo SSL certificate with 2048-bit encryption so that your personal and billing information are always protected. After being used to process your order, credit card information is discarded and never stored online.
