Sponsorship Information

Welcome to one of the most exciting places within the iRun family, our Sponsorship Program. This is exciting because it is where our identity of the iRun brand and the identity of our athletes intersect and merge.

We look at the world of sponsorship more as Brand Ambassadors than we do simply in a sponsor-sponsored relationship. In this type of relationship, both parties understand that they have responsibilities to uphold the image, reputation and credibility of each other.

Our sponsored athletes have the responsibility to never do anything that would tarnish, degrade or otherwise discredit the iRun brand and in return, we reciprocate that by producing the highest quality products on the market and providing you a solid brand in which you can be proud to be associated.

What types of sponsorships are available?


At iRun, we offer three distinct levels of Sponsorship:

  1. 1Professional Athlete level - APPLY NOW!
  2. 2Elite Athlete level - APPLY NOW!
  3. 3Age Groupers - APPLY NOW!

  1. Professional Athlete: This level of sponsorship is available to the 1% of the athletic world that has achieved professional status through an accrediting agency. Based on your standingsas a professional athlete, you are entitled to a package of benefits which are commensurate to the hard work, sacrifice and dedication that you have spent a lifetime achieving. Click here to apply online!

  2. Elite Athlete: This level of sponsorship is available for those athletes that have separated themselves from the pack, as they do in competition. Our Elite Athletes are not quite at the professional level or are professionals that are just taking a break from the grind. We understand the amount of work that it takes to ascend to this level of competitive excellence and offer you a package of benefits that will meet and exceed your expectations. Click here to apply online!

  3. Age Grouper: 95% of the athletes that compete are what we call “Age Groupers” and this is why our founder created our Age Grouper level of sponsorship. This level of sponsorship provides the weekend warrior the opportunity to become a part of a global brand. We all wear t-shirts, hats or other items that bear the brand of a company. Why not wear a brand that YOU represent in a meaningful way and allows you to show the world who you are, what you do and why you are important. Our founder puts it this way:

    "99% of the arrow is behind the tip, which is our company’s philosophy in regards to athletes. It is the shaft that gives the arrow its identity and NOT the tip…because without the arrow, the arrow tip would just be a really small knife!"

    You, the Age Group Athlete makes every race possible, every event a success and every cause worth fighting for. Without you, there would only be about 10 people at every race. iRun wants to thank you for your time, sacrifice and support by offering you our Age Grouper level of sponsorship which provides you with discounts on our entire family of iRun products. Click here to apply online!
